Welcome to Chat Index

Chat Index is a fast, flexible, and highly customizable AI chating application
designed for users who like to keep multiple conversations organized and
accessible. Its tab-based interface makes switching between chats feel
natural, smooth, and immediate.

The app provides powerful customization options while keeping the default
setup simple and ready to use. You can tailor almost every part of the
experience — from selecting custom models through OpenRouter API names, to
choosing themes from a built-in library or defining your own with JSON, to
creating custom keyboard shortcuts and attaching scripts to them.

Chat Index focuses on responsiveness, clarity, and control. Whether you
prefer a clean, minimal layout or a fully personalized workspace, the
interface adapts to the way you work. Multiple chats, flexible layouts,
custom themes, configurable models, and shortcut scripting all come
together to support a workspace that stays fast and reliable even as your
workflow expands.

This documentation will guide you through the app’s features,
customization tools, and interfaces, helping you get started quickly and
explore the parts that matter most to you.
